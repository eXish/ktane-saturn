Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes Mod Saturn

On the Subject of Saturn

Try not to hit your head on any rocks along the way.

This module shows a small scale version of the planet Saturn. If there is a standard module background, or the planet shown is not Saturn, you are looking at the wrong module.

To solve this module, navigate through the maze below of the planet’s rings to the end destination. Your current position in the maze is the white sphere and the end destination is the green sphere. Hovering over a sphere will display exact coordinates of the sphere in the format of number of cells up then right (0-indexed) starting from the bottommost cell of the innermost ring at the north of the maze. The part of the rings that is closest to the brown sphere is the north of the maze.

Pressing the four spheres surrounding the planet in clockwise order starting from the brown sphere will move you up, right, down, and left within the current ring. To move out or in a ring press the spheres above or below the planet respectively. Pressing the sphere near the status light will make the planet disappear, until said sphere is pressed again.

Hitting a wall in the maze or leaving the maze will result in a strike.
